## Il Modello ICONIQ

### "Silicon Valley Billionaires Club"

ICONIQ opera come hybrid tra:
- **Family office**: Wealth management per ~300 famiglie
- **VC fund**: Growth investments con deal flow esclusivo
- **Network**: Accesso a founder-to-founder connections

### Vantaggi competitivi

| Aspetto | Dettaglio |
|---------|-----------|
| **Deal flow** | Clienti sono founder, introducono deal |
| **Co-investment** | Clienti investono insieme nei deals |
| **Network effect** | Zuckerberg introduce altri founder |
| **Discretion** | Massima riservatezza |

### ICONIQ Labs (Philanthropy)

Co-Labs: fondi collaborativi per filantropia
- Climate equity
- Economic mobility
- Multi-year grants pooled
