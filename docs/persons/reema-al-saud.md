# Reema bint Bandar Al Saud (1975)

> First female Saudi ambassador to the United States, daughter of Prince Bandar bin Sultan, key figure in Saudi Vision 2030 women's reform

## Dati Essenziali

| Aspetto | Dettaglio |
|---------|-----------|
| **Nascita** | 15 febbraio 1975, Riyadh |
| **Famiglia** | Casa dei Saud, pronipote di Ibn Saud |
| **Padre** | Bandar bin Sultan (ambasciatore SAU in USA 1983-2005) |
| **Madre** | Haifa bint Faisal |

## Carriera e Ruoli

### Timeline Professionale

| Periodo | Ruolo | Organizzazione |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **2007-2015** | CEO | Alfa International (Harvey Nichols Riyadh) |
| **2013** | Founder | Alf Khair (social enterprise) |
| **2016** | VP Women's Affairs | Saudi General Sports Authority |
| **2017** | Deputy of Development and Planning | Saudi General Sports Authority |
| **2018** | President | Mass Participation Federation |
| **2019-** | Ambassador to the United States | Kingdom of Saudi Arabia |
