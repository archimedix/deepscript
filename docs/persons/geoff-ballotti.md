## Carriera e Ruoli

### Timeline Professionale

| Periodo | Ruolo | Organizzazione |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **pre-1989** | Banking Officer, Commercial Real Estate | Bank of New England |
| **pre-1989** | Dishwasher | Hotel Le Méridien, Boston |
| **1989–2008** | Managing Director, Ciga SpA (Italia) | [Starwood Hotels](../company/starwood-hotels.md) |
| **1989–2008** | SVP Southern Europe | [Starwood Hotels](../company/starwood-hotels.md) |
| **1989–2008** | EVP Operations | [Starwood Hotels](../company/starwood-hotels.md) |
| **1989–2008** | President North America | [Starwood Hotels](../company/starwood-hotels.md) |
| **2008–2012** | CEO | Wyndham Destinations |
| **2012–2018** | President & CEO | Wyndham Hotel Group |
| **2018–** | President & CEO | [Wyndham Hotels & Resorts](../company/wyndham.md) |

### Connessione italiana

In qualità di Managing Director di **Ciga SpA** in Italia, Ballotti ha gestito hotel storici come il **Gritti Palace** e l'**Hotel Danieli** a Venezia — due delle proprietà alberghiere più iconiche d'Italia.
