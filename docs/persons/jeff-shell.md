## Paramount-Skydance Deal (2024-2025)

### Struttura

- **David Ellison**: Chairman & CEO (50% voting rights)
- **Larry Ellison** (padre): 27.5% voting rights
- **RedBird Capital**: 22.5% voting rights
- **Jeff Shell**: President (operazioni day-to-day)

### Finanziamento

| Investitore | Importo |
|-------------|---------|
| Famiglia Ellison | $6 miliardi |
| RedBird Capital | $2 miliardi |

### Obiettivi Shell

- $2 miliardi cost synergies
- Streaming break-even entro 18 mesi
