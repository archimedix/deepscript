### FCC Chairman (2009-2013)

Principali iniziative:
- **Connect America Fund**: riforma del Universal Service Fund da telefono a broadband
- **Incentive Auctions**: riallocazione spettro da broadcast a mobile broadband
- **AT&T/T-Mobile merger**: bloccato dalla FCC

### Pattern: Revolving Door

```
FCC Chief Counsel (1994-97)
        │
        v
Private Sector - IAC (1997-2009)
        │
        v
FCC Chairman (2009-13) ← Obama nomination
        │
        v
Carlyle Group Partner (2014-23)
        │
        v
Carlyle Senior Advisor (2024-)
```

Classico pattern regulator → private equity.
