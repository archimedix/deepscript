|---------|-----------|
| **Nascita** | 1964, Toronto, Canada |

| **Net Worth** | ~$5B (2025) |

## Formazione

| Periodo | Istituzione | Dettaglio |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| **1982-1987** | [Princeton University](../university/princeton.md) | BSE Electrical Engineering and Computer Science |

**Focus accademico:** Robotics e Artificial Intelligence. Lavoro al Princeton Robotics Lab su symbolic AI, machine vision, neural networks, autonomous vehicles.

## Carriera e Ruoli

### Timeline Professionale

| Periodo | Ruolo | Organizzazione |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **1987-~1990** | Research Manager | Princeton Robotics and Expert Systems Laboratory |
| **~1990s** | Director NY Office | Blacksmith Software Consulting |
| **~1990s** | Developer | tomandandy music (autonomous composition tool) |
| **~1990s** | AI Consultant | Vision Applications Inc. (autonomous mobile robots) |
| **~1990s** | Consultant | eMagine (Identrus cryptographic payments project) |
| **~2000s** | Co-founder | Hedge fund (con partner) |
| **~2000s** | VP Technology | [Goldman Sachs](../company/goldman-sachs.md) Private Wealth Management |
| **~2012-2014** | Music industry | Kingston, Jamaica |
| **2014** | Co-founder, COO | [Ethereum Foundation](../foundation/ethereum-foundation.md) (EthSuisse) |
| **2015-oggi** | Founder, CEO | [ConsenSys](../company/consensys.md) |

### La Transizione a Blockchain

| Anno | Evento |
|------|--------|
| **2011** | Legge il Bitcoin whitepaper di Satoshi Nakamoto |
| **~2012** | Si trasferisce in Giamaica per progetto musicale |
| **Late 2013** | Legge l'Ethereum whitepaper di Vitalik Buterin |
| **Early 2014** | Co-fonda Ethereum, diventa COO di EthSuisse |
| **2014** | ICO Ethereum raccoglie $18M |
| **2015** | Fonda ConsenSys dopo il lancio mainnet |

## ConsenSys

### Overview

| Metrica | Valore |
|---------|--------|
| **Fondata** | 2015 |
| **Sede** | Brooklyn, New York |
| **Valutazione** | $7B (Series D, 2022) |
| **Funding totale** | $725M |
| **Prodotti chiave** | MetaMask, Infura |

### Prodotti Principali

| Prodotto | Descrizione | Impatto |
|----------|-------------|---------|
| **MetaMask** | Crypto wallet browser/mobile | 30M+ MAU, ~80-90% market share Web3 |
| **Infura** | API per sviluppatori Ethereum | 430k developers, $1T+ transazioni annue |

### Funding History

| Round | Anno | Importo | Valutazione | Lead Investors |
|-------|------|---------|-------------|----------------|
| Series A | 2019 | $10M | - | - |
| Series B | 2021 | ~$65M | - | - |
| Series C | 2021 | $200M | $3.2B | - |
| Series D | 2022 | $450M | $7B | ParaFi Capital, Microsoft, Temasek, SoftBank |

### Sviluppi 2025

- IPO in preparazione (JPMorgan, Goldman Sachs come underwriters)
- MetaMask: integrazione Polymarket, lancio token MASK
- MetaMask Rewards con $30M in allocazioni LINEA
