## La Presentazione ONU (5 Febbraio 2003)

### I Fatti

| Aspetto | Dettaglio |
|---------|-----------|
| **Durata** | 76 minuti |
| **Audience** | Consiglio di Sicurezza ONU |
| **Contenuto** | "Prove" di WMD irachene |
| **Menzioni "WMD"** | 17 volte |
| **Fonti** | "Curveball" (inaffidabile), intelligence manipolata |
| **Risultato** | Nessuna WMD mai trovata |

### Le 254 False Dichiarazioni

| Categoria | Numero |
|-----------|--------|
| **WMD** | 244 |
| **Legami Al-Qaeda** | 10 |
| **Totale** | 254 |

*Fonte: [Center for Public Integrity](https://publicintegrity.org/politics/false-pretenses/)*

### Le Ammissioni

> "I, of course, regret the U.N. speech that I gave... But we thought it was correct at the time."
> — Colin Powell

> "I was more than embarrassed. I was mortified."
> — Colin Powell, Bloomberg 2017

> "It's a blot. I'm the one who presented it on behalf of the United States to the world, and [it] will always be a part of my record. It was painful. It's painful now."
> — Colin Powell, 2005

### Il Ruolo di Lawrence Wilkerson

Il suo Chief of Staff Lawrence Wilkerson ammise poi:

> — Lawrence Wilkerson
