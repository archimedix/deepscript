## Carriera e Ruoli

### Timeline Professionale

| Periodo | Ruolo | Organizzazione |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **1998-2005** | Intern → Press Secretary → Legislative Director | Office of John Boehner |
| **2005** | Special Assistant to the President | National Economic Council, White House |
| **2006-2010** | Deputy Chief of Staff, Policy Director | Office of John Boehner |
| **2010-2015** | Chief of Staff | Speaker John Boehner |
| **2016-2018** | CEO | American Investment Council |
| **2018-** | CEO | [American Petroleum Institute](../forum/american-petroleum-institute.md) |

### Legislazione Chiave

Durante gli anni con Boehner, Sommers ha negoziato:
- TARP (2008) - Troubled Asset Relief Program
- Fiscal Cliff resolution (2013)
- Bipartisan Budget Agreement (2015)
- Trade Promotion Authority (2015)
