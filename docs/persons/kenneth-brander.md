## Carriera e Ruoli

### Timeline Professionale

| Periodo | Ruolo | Organizzazione |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **~1986-1990** | Assistant Rabbi | Lincoln Square Synagogue, New York |
| **1990-1991** | Acting Rabbi | Lincoln Square Synagogue, New York |
| **1991-2005** | Senior Rabbi | Boca Raton Synagogue, Florida |
| **2005-2018** | VP University and Community Life; Dean CJF | [Yeshiva University](../university/yeshiva-university.md) |
| **2018-** | President e Rosh HaYeshiva | [Ohr Torah Stone](../religious-institution/ohr-torah-stone.md) |

### Boca Raton Synagogue (1991-2005)

In 14 anni porta la comunita' da 60 a oltre 600 famiglie, una delle crescite piu' rapide nel Modern Orthodox americano. Fonda la Katz Yeshiva High School (prima scuola ebraica superiore tra North Miami Beach e Atlanta).

### Yeshiva University (2005-2018)

Vice President for University and Community Life e primo Dean del Center for the Jewish Future (CJF).

### Ohr Torah Stone (2018-)

Succede a [Shlomo Riskin](shlomo-riskin.md) come President e Rosh HaYeshiva della rete di 32 istituzioni educative.
