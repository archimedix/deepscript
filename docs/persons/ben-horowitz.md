### Timeline Professionale

| Periodo | Ruolo | Organizzazione |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **1990-1995** | Engineer | Silicon Graphics |
| **1995-1998** | Product Manager, poi VP | [Netscape](../company/netscape.md) |
| **1998-1999** | VP eCommerce Division | AOL |
| **1999-2002** | Co-founder, CEO | Loudcloud |
| **2002-2007** | CEO | Opsware (ex Loudcloud) |

### Tappe Chiave

| Anno | Evento |

| **2019** | Pubblica "What You Do Is Who You Are" |
