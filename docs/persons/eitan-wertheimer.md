## Carriera e Ruoli

### Timeline Professionale

| Periodo | Ruolo | Organizzazione |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **1970s** | Operaio, poi manager | [ISCAR](../company/iscar.md) |
| **1988** | Chairman | ISCAR / IMC Group |
| **1995** | CEO | ISCAR (dopo incidente padre) |
| **2006-2022** | Board member | [Berkshire Hathaway](../company/berkshire-hathaway.md) |

### L'Acquisizione Berkshire

Eitan Wertheimer dedico' due anni alla ricerca dell'acquirente ideale per ISCAR:

> "I spent two years researching who would be the best person to buy the business and decided that Warren Buffett was the only one. If Warren had not wanted to buy ISCAR, then I don't think we would have sold it."

| Anno | Evento | Valore |
|------|--------|--------|
| **2006** | Vendita 80% a Berkshire | $4 miliardi |
| **2013** | Vendita restante 20% | $2.05 miliardi |

**Significato storico**: Prima acquisizione di Berkshire Hathaway in Israele e prima grande acquisizione fuori dagli USA.
