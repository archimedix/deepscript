### "Secretary of Everything" (NYT)

Ruoli simultanei nel 2025:
- Secretary of State
- Acting National Security Advisor
- Acting USAID Administrator
- Acting Archivist of the United States

Ultima volta che una persona ha ricoperto State + NSA: Henry Kissinger (1973-75).
