### Il Record Unico

James Baker e' l'**unico americano** ad aver servito come:
1. **Secretary of State** (61st, 1989-1992)
2. **Secretary of the Treasury** (67th, 1985-1988)
3. **White House Chief of Staff** (10th e 16th, 1981-1985, 1992-1993)

> "The most important unelected official in America since World War II."
> — Antony Blinken, 2024

## L'Eredita'

James Baker rappresenta l'apice dell'establishment repubblicano texano: dinastia legale, servizio militare, successo negli affari, potere politico ai massimi livelli. La sua capacita' di costruire coalizioni - dalla riforma fiscale all'alleanza per Desert Storm - rimane un modello di diplomazia pragmatica.

> "Since the death of Henry Kissinger in 2023, he is currently the oldest living former United States secretary of state, as well as the earliest serving and only living former secretary to have served in the 20th century."
