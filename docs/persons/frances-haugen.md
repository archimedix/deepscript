### Temi Principali

| Tema | Rivelazione |
|------|-------------|
| **Instagram e adolescenti** | Ricerche interne mostravano danni alla salute mentale |

## Testimonianze

### Senato USA (5 ottobre 2021)

> "I'm here today because I believe Facebook's products harm children, stoke division, and weaken our democracy."

> "The company's leadership knows how to make Facebook and Instagram safer, but won't make the necessary changes because they have put their astronomical profits before people."

> "Almost no one outside of Facebook knows what happens inside of Facebook. The company intentionally hides vital information from the public."

### Su Zuckerberg

> "There are no similarly powerful companies that are as unilaterally controlled."

Haugen sottolineo' che la struttura di voto di Facebook rende Zuckerberg "accountable only to himself".
