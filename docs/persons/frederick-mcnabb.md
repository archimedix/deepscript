### Pattern di Potere

- **Vanguard lifer**: 32 anni nella stessa azienda (1986-2018), da entry level a Chairman del piu' grande asset manager al mondo (~$9T AUM)
- **Cross-sector boards**: asset management (Vanguard) → tech (IBM) + healthcare (UnitedHealth)
- **Wharton network**: MBA dalla business school piu' influente di Wall Street
- **Post-retirement pivot**: da asset management a advisory in private equity (Permira) e venture capital (Venrock)
