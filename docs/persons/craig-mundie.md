## Carriera e Ruoli

### Timeline Professionale

| Periodo | Ruolo | Organizzazione |
|---------|-------|----------------|
| **1970** | OS Developer | Systems Equipment Corporation |
| **1970s** | Director Advanced Development | Data General Corporation |
| **1982-1992** | Co-Founder, CEO | Alliant Computer Systems |
| **1992-2006** | Various executive roles | Microsoft |
| **2006-2012** | Chief Research and Strategy Officer | Microsoft |
| **2012-2014** | Senior Advisor to CEO | Microsoft |
| **2015-2016** | Co-Executive Chairman | Bridgewater Associates |
| **2014-oggi** | President | Mundie & Associates |

### Microsoft (1992-2014)

Carriera 22 anni in Microsoft:
- **1992**: Entra nella consumer platforms division
- **1997**: Supervisiona acquisizione WebTV Networks
- **2006**: Quando Bill Gates si ritira, divide i suoi compiti con Ray Ozzie
- **2006-2012**: Chief Research and Strategy Officer - supervisiona Microsoft Research
- **2012-2014**: Senior Advisor to CEO Steve Ballmer

Responsabilita' chiave:
- Microsoft Research (uno dei piu' grandi centri di ricerca informatica al mondo)
- Intellectual Property Strategy
- Technology Policy
- Trustworthy Computing
- Principal technology-policy liaison con governi USA e stranieri (focus: Cina, India, Russia)

### Bridgewater Associates (2015-2016)

Co-Executive Chairman del piu' grande hedge fund al mondo, insieme a Eileen Murray.
