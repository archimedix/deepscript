### Media Integration

### Strategic Vision

Ahuja rappresenta il nuovo modello di executive Hollywood:
- Background finance (Wharton, McKinsey, investment banking)
- Expertise M&A e integrazione
- Focus su streaming e nuove tecnologie
- Continuita' manageriale (groomed successor)
