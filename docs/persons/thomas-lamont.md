## Potere e Influenza

### Giudizio di Ferdinand Lundberg (1937)

> "Thomas Lamont has exercised more power for twenty years in the western hemisphere, has put into effect more final decisions from which there has been no appeal, than any other person. Lamont, in short, has been the First Consul de facto in the invisible Directory of postwar high finance and politics, a man consulted by presidents, prime ministers, governors of central banks."

## Italy America Society

Lamont fu tra i membri piu' attivi della Italy America Society (IAS):
- Lobbying organization con legami a State Department e Wall Street
- Promosse Mussolini come "ideal anti-Bolshevik" dopo Marcia su Roma
- Legittimo' relazioni finanziarie USA-regime fascista
