## Figure Chiave - Dettaglio

### Donna Langley, DBE
- **Ruolo attuale**: Chairman NBCUniversal Entertainment & Studios (2024-)
- **Background**: Production executive New Line Cinema, poi 24 anni Universal Pictures
- **Record**: Unica donna a guidare legacy Hollywood studio
- **Onorificenze**: Dame Commander of the Order of the British Empire (2020)
- **Successi**: Oppenheimer (Oscar 2024), Wicked, Fast & Furious franchise
- **Board**: AFI, Motion Picture Association, Vital Voices
- **Mentor**: [Michael De Luca](../persons/michael-de-luca.md)

### Cesar Conde
- **Ruolo attuale**: Chairman NBCUniversal News Group (2020-)
- **Background**: White House Fellow (Colin Powell 2002-03), President Univision (2009-2013)
- **Record**: Primo ispanico a guidare major English-language news org, piu' giovane network president USA (35 anni)
- **Iniziative**: Fifty Percent Challenge (diversity), NBCU Academy ($6.5M per journalism training)
- **Forum**: WEF (YGL 2012), CFR, Aspen Institute
- **Board**: Walmart, PepsiCo, Paley Center

### Mark Lazarus (fino 2025)
- **Ruolo**: Chairman NBCUniversal Media Group (2023-2025), ora CEO Versant
- **Background**: Dinastia broadcasting (padre VP ABC Sports, fratelli a NBC/ESPN)
- **Reclutato da**: [Dick Ebersol](../persons/dick-ebersol.md) (2010)
- **Successi**: Sunday Night Football #1 primetime per 12 anni consecutivi
- **Onorificenze**: Sports Broadcasting Hall of Fame (2024)

### Jeff Shell (2020-2023)
- **Ruolo**: CEO NBCUniversal
- **Background**: Harvard MBA, CEO Gemstar TV Guide, Chairman BBG/USAGM (Obama)
- **Uscita**: Aprile 2023 dopo indagine interna
- **Attuale**: President Paramount Skydance (2025-)
- **Forum**: WEF

### David Zaslav (fino 2006)
- **Ruolo**: Cable Division President
- **Contributo**: Lancio CNBC, MSNBC
- **Attuale**: CEO Warner Bros. Discovery

## Spin-off Versant (2025-2026)

Nel novembre 2024 Comcast annuncia spin-off delle cable networks:

| Campo | Dettaglio |
|-------|-----------|
| **Nome** | Versant (annunciato maggio 2025) |
| **CEO** | [Mark Lazarus](../persons/mark-lazarus.md) |
| **Completamento** | 2 gennaio 2026 |
| **IPO** | 5 gennaio 2026, Nasdaq (VSNT) |
| **Apertura** | $45.17/share |
| **Revenue target 2026** | $6.6 miliardi |

**Asset trasferiti a Versant:**
- USA Network, CNBC, MS NOW (ex MSNBC), Golf Channel, Oxygen, E!, Syfy
- Fandango, Rotten Tomatoes, GolfNow, SportsEngine
