## Pattern Identificati

### 1. DARPA → Biotech Pipeline

```
DARPA (2013)
    |
    +-- $25M grant mRNA
    |
    v
MODERNA (2010-2020)
    |
    +-- 10 anni R&D senza prodotti
    +-- $2B+ venture capital
    |
    v
COVID-19 (2020)
```
