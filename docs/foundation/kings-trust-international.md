## Struttura e Governance

### Struttura Gruppo

The King's Trust International e' parte del **King's Trust Group**, network globale di charity fondate da Re Carlo III:
- The King's Trust (UK)
- The King's Trust International
- The King's Trust Australia
- The King's Trust Canada
- The King's Trust New Zealand
- The King's Trust America (USA)

Il **King's Trust Group Company** e' il parent charity e sole member di KTI.
