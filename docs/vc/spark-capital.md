## Investment Strategy

### Focus

| Settore | Note |
|---------|------|
| Consumer Internet | Twitter, Tumblr, Discord |
| Enterprise Software | Slack, Deel |
| Fintech | Affirm, Coinbase |
| AI | Anthropic |
| Hardware/Deep Tech | Santo Politi focus |
| Gaming | Nabeel Hyatt background |

### Filosofia

- **Stage**: Primarily Series A
- **Geography**: USA-focused
- **Approach**: Product-first evaluation (Nabeel Hyatt: "learn more from experiencing their choices with the product than from a pitch")
- **Fund structure**: Separate early-stage e growth funds
