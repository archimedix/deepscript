## Strategia

### Il Modello Formation

| Fase | Azione |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Acquisire nursing homes distressed/undervalued |
| 2 | Separare real estate da operating company |
| 3 | Contenere liability (multiple entities) |
| 4 | "Restore profitability" |
| 5 | Vendere a prezzo maggiorato |

## Pattern Rilevante

Formation Capital rappresenta il **PE healthcare playbook**:

1. **Target distressed** - nursing homes in difficolta'
2. **Separate ownership** - real estate vs operations
3. **Liability shield** - multiple entities
4. **Staff cuts** - "restore profitability"
5. **Exit** - vendita o IPO a multiplo maggiore

### Quote Whitman

> "Quality of care equals quality financial outcomes... if you think you're going to cut and burn and slash, and understaff and under-provide care to make money in this business, it's just not realistic."

**Contraddizione**: le azioni di Formation (Genesis bankruptcy, NY Times investigation) contraddicono questa affermazione.
