## Fondatore: Gerry Cardinale

| Dato | Dettaglio |
|------|-----------|
| **Nato** | 8 maggio 1967 |
| **Nazionalita'** | USA (origini italiane) |
| **Education** | Harvard (Magna Cum Laude), Oxford (Rhodes Scholar) |
| **Goldman Sachs** | 1992-2012, Partner dal 2004 |
| **RedBird** | Fondatore 2014 |

### Carriera Goldman Sachs

- **1992**: Entra in Goldman Sachs
- **1994**: Merchant Banking Division
- **1994-1997**: Hong Kong, China, Singapore
- **2004**: Eletto Partner
- **2001**: Crea YES Network con George Steinbrenner
- **2008**: Crea Legends Hospitality (Yankees + Jerry Jones)
- **2012**: Exit, fonda RedBird

### Philosophy

> "Pencils down on all major U.S. sports team acquisitions right now as valuations soar... There needs to be some normalization in what I see as a bit of an asset bubble." (2025)
