## Fondazione e Storia

| Dato | Dettaglio |
|------|-----------|
| **Origini** | Hospitality Franchise Systems (HFS), 1990 |
| **Merger** | HFS + CUC International → Cendant Corporation (1997) |
| **Spin-off 1** | Cendant → Wyndham Worldwide (2006) |
| **Spin-off 2** | Wyndham Worldwide → Wyndham Hotels & Resorts (2018) |
| **Architetto** | Stephen P. Holmes, ha guidato tutte le transizioni |

### Timeline Storica

| Anno | Evento |
|------|--------|
| **1990** | Fondazione di Hospitality Franchise Systems (HFS) |
| **1995** | HFS acquisisce Days Inn, Howard Johnson, Ramada, Super 8 |
| **1997** | HFS + CUC International → Cendant Corporation |
| **2005** | Cendant acquisisce brand Wyndham da Blackstone Group |
| **2006** | Spin-off: Cendant → Wyndham Worldwide (CEO: Stephen Holmes) |
| **2018** | Spin-off: Wyndham Worldwide → Wyndham Hotels & Resorts (CEO: Geoff Ballotti) |
