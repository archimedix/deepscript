## Pattern di Potere

### 2025 Board Refresh: Political Alignment

Nel 2025, Meta ha aggiunto 5 nuovi board members con chiaro profilo:
- **John Elkann**: Old European money (Agnelli), ex-Bilderberg inner circle
- **Dina Powell**: Republican operative, Goldman Sachs, Saudi/Gulf connections
- **Dana White**: Trump ally, UFC CEO
- **Patrick Collison**: Tech founder (Stripe), payments infrastructure
- **Charlie Songhurst**: Ex-Microsoft, tech investor

Pattern: spostamento verso establishment Republican/Trump-aligned dopo anni di tensioni con conservatori USA.

### Dual-Class Control

Zuckerberg mantiene controllo assoluto nonostante minoranza economica:
- ~13% azioni = ~60% voti
- Nessuna possibilita' di takeover ostile
- Board e' advisory, non può rimuovere CEO

### Revolving Door Government-Tech

| Persona | Da | A | Pattern |
|---------|-----|-----|---------|
| Nick Clegg | Deputy PM UK | President Meta | Regulator → Regulated |
| Dina Powell | NSC, State Dept | Board Meta | National Security → Big Tech |
| Robert Kimmitt | Treasury, CIA Advisory | Lead Director | Intelligence-Finance → Tech Governance |
