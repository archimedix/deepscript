## Fondazione e Storia

| Dato | Dettaglio |
|------|-----------|
| **Fondazione** | 3 October 1953 |
| **Fondatore** | Governo Vargas |
| **Contesto** | Nationalist movement "O Petróleo é Nosso" (The Oil is Ours) |
| **Obiettivo dichiarato** | State monopoly over Brazilian oil production |

### Timeline Storica

| Anno | Evento |
|------|--------|
| **1953** | Founded under President Getúlio Vargas with legal monopoly |
| **1968** | Established Petroquisa (petrochemicals subsidiary) |
| **1997** | Oil sector liberalized; ANP created as regulator |
| **1998** | Transpetro logistics subsidiary established |
| **2006** | Tupi pre-salt field discovery - game changer |
| **2010** | Record $70B stock offering for pre-salt development |
| **2014** | Lava Jato (Operation Car Wash) investigation begins |
| **2015** | $17B write-down; CEO Maria das Graças Foster resigns |
| **2018** | DOJ/SEC settlement: $853M fine |
| **2021** | Petrobras Distribuidora spun off as Vibra Energia |
| **2024** | CEO Jean Paul Prates fired; Magda Chambriard appointed |

## Lava Jato Scandal

### Overview

Operation Car Wash (2014-2021) exposed systematic corruption at Petrobras involving:
- Kickbacks from construction contractors (Odebrecht, OAS, etc.)
- Political party financing (PT, PMDB, PP)
- Overvalued contracts and money laundering

### Financial Impact

| Item | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Write-downs (2015) | $17B |
| DOJ/SEC fine (2018) | $853M |
| Class-action settlement | $2.6B |
| Estimated bribes | $2.1B |

### Key Figures Implicated

- **Marcelo Odebrecht**: CEO Norberto Odebrecht, sentenced
- **Dilma Rousseff**: Impeached 2016 (budget manipulation)
- **Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva**: Convicted (later annulled)
