## Pattern di Potere

### US-China Tech Decoupling

TikTok rappresenta il caso emblematico del decoupling tech:
- **Security concerns**: Accesso dati utenti USA da CCP
- **Algorithm control**: Recommendation engine come strumento di influenza
- **Precedent**: Prima app consumer bannata per national security

### Bipartisan Consensus

| Aspetto | Dettaglio |
|---------|-----------|
| **Raro accordo** | Democrats + Republicans united |
| **Vote** | PAFACA passed with overwhelming majority |
| **Supreme Court** | Unanimous decision (no dissents) |
| **Pattern** | China policy unico tema bipartisan |

### PE/VC Geopolitical Play

Il deal TikTok USA mostra come PE/VC navigano geopolitics:
- **Susquehanna** (Jeff Yass): 15% ByteDance, major GOP donor
- **Oracle** (Larry Ellison): Trump ally, tech infrastructure
- **MGX**: Abu Dhabi SWF come neutral bridge
- **a16z**: Marc Andreessen, Trump supporter
