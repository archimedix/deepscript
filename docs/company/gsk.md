## Pattern Identificati

### 1. Tech-Pharma Board Crossover

Walmsley (GSK CEO) in Microsoft board. Pattern di cross-pollination tra Big Tech e Big Pharma.

### 2. CFO-to-Chairman Pipeline

Symonds: CFO AstraZeneca → CFO Novartis → Chairman GSK. Competenze finanziarie portano alla leadership.

### 3. Closed Executive Club

Miels ha lavorato in 4 Big Pharma. Symonds CFO in 2 e Chairman in 1. Il settore pharma e' un club chiuso con executive che ruotano.

### 4. Focus Strategy

Spin-off Haleon (2022) mirror Novartis spin-off Sandoz (2023). Entrambe cercano "pure-play biopharma".

### 5. First Female CEO

Walmsley e' stata prima donna CEO di una Big Pharma (2017). Background consumer (L'Oreal), non scientist.
