## Il Caso Elizabeth Holmes

### Timeline

| Data | Evento |
|------|--------|
| **2015** | Jeffrey Flier invita Holmes al Board of Fellows |
| **2015** | Wall Street Journal pubblica expose' su Theranos |
| **2015-16** | Dibattito interno su dimissioni Holmes |
| **2016** | Flier chiede dimissioni a Holmes |
| **2022** | Holmes condannata per frode |

### Lezioni Apprese

> "In retrospect we made a mistake in asking her to join despite her winning awards and gaining broad attention, we corrected the mistake in a reasonable time period allowing facts to emerge." - Jeffrey Flier

**Criticita':**
- Mancanza di due diligence
- Phyllis Gardner (Stanford) aveva avvertito Flier
- Board members inizialmente non convinti della frode

## Pattern Identificati

### 1. Board of Fellows come Network Hub

```
BOARD OF FELLOWS (Advisory)
         |
         +---> Pharma CEOs (Narasimhan)
         |
         +---> Ex-Pharma Executives (Rosenblatt)
         |
         +---> Biotech Founders (Bertarelli)
         |
         +---> Healthcare Investors (Frist)
```

### 2. Alumni → Industry Leadership Pipeline

HMS produce sistematicamente leadership per:
- Big Pharma (Novartis, Merck)
- Biotech (Verve, Momenta, Dendreon)
- Governo (NIH, FDA, World Bank)

Bertarelli (Svizzera) come Chairman Board of Fellows:
- Serono → Merck Serono
