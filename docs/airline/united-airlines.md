## Fondazione e Storia

| Dato | Dettaglio |
|------|-----------|
| **Fondazione** | 6 aprile 1926 |
| **Nome originale** | Varney Air Lines |
| **Fondatore** | Walter Varney |
| **Predecessori** | Boeing Air Transport, Pacific Air Transport, National Air Transport |

### Timeline Storica

| Anno | Evento |
|------|--------|
| **1926** | Fondata come Varney Air Lines |
| **1931** | Formazione United Air Lines da fusione 4 compagnie |
| **1934** | Separazione da Boeing (Air Mail Act) |
| **1961** | Acquisizione Capital Airlines |
| **1997** | Membro fondatore Star Alliance |
| **2002-2006** | Chapter 11 reorganization |
| **2010** | Fusione con Continental Airlines |
| **2016** | Scott Kirby President (da American Airlines) |
| **2020** | Scott Kirby CEO |
| **2021** | Edward Philip Chairman |

## Alleanze e Partnership

### Star Alliance

United e' membro fondatore di [Star Alliance](../forum/star-alliance.md) dal 1997. Scott Kirby e' Chairman dello Star Alliance Chief Executive Board.

### Airlines for America (A4A)

Membro di [Airlines for America](../think-tank/airlines-for-america.md), lobby dell'industria aerea USA.

## Connessioni PowerLink

### Network figure chiave

**[J. Scott Kirby](../persons/scott-kirby.md)** - CEO dal 2020, veterano industria aerea (30+ anni):
- Chairman [Star Alliance](../forum/star-alliance.md) Chief Executive Board (2020-)
- Chairman [Airlines for America (A4A)](../think-tank/airlines-for-america.md)
- [IATA](../agency/iata.md) Board of Governors
- [Business Roundtable](../thinktank/business-roundtable.md) member
- Architetto consolidamento: America West→US Airways (2005), US Airways→American (2013)
- Alumni [US Air Force Academy](../university/us-air-force-academy.md) (BS 1989), [GWU](../university/george-washington-university.md) (MS)

**[Edward M. Philip](../persons/edward-philip.md)** - Chairman dal 2021, ponte tech-healthcare-aviation:
- Board United dal 2016, Lead Director 2020-2021
- Co-fondatore [Lycos](../company/lycos.md) (President/COO/CFO, 1996-2000) - pioniere dot-com
- Ex COO [Partners in Health](../foundation/partners-in-health.md) (2013-2017) - healthcare non-profit globale
- Ex VP Finance [Walt Disney](../media/walt-disney.md) (1991-1995)
- Board: [BRP Inc.](../company/brp.md), [Blade Air Mobility](../company/blade-air-mobility.md)
- Alumni [Vanderbilt](../university/vanderbilt.md), [Harvard MBA](../university/harvard.md)

**[Walter Isaacson](../persons/walter-isaacson.md)** - Board member, connettore elite media-tech:
- Ex President & CEO [Aspen Institute](../thinktank/aspen-institute.md) (2003-2018)
- Ex Chairman & CEO [CNN](../media/cnn.md) (2001-2003)
- Ex Managing Editor [TIME Magazine](../media/time-magazine.md) (1996-2001)
- Advisory Partner [Perella Weinberg Partners](../bank/perella-weinberg.md) (2020-)
- Professor [Tulane University](../university/tulane.md) (2018-)
- Rhodes Scholar ([Harvard](../university/harvard.md), [Oxford](../university/oxford.md))
- Biografo: Steve Jobs, Elon Musk, Jennifer Doudna

**[James Whitehurst](../persons/james-whitehurst.md)** - Board member, crossover aviation-tech:
- Ex President [IBM](../company/ibm.md) (2020-2021)

| **Tech** | Philip, Whitehurst, Isaacson | Philip: Lycos founder; Whitehurst: Red Hat, IBM, Unity; Isaacson: biografo tech |
