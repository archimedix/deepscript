## Glossario

| Termine | Definizione |
|---------|-------------|
| **Morehead-Cain** | Prima borsa di merito negli USA (1945). 31 dei 34 Rhodes Scholar UNC erano Morehead-Cain |
| **Tiger Cubs** | Hedge fund manager formati da Julian Robertson a Tiger Management, poi indipendenti |
| **CRO** | Contract Research Organization - aziende che conducono trial clinici per conto di pharma |
| **TISS** | Triangle Institute for Security Studies - consorzio UNC-Duke-NCSU per intelligence e sicurezza |

### Timeline Storica

| Anno | Evento |
|------|--------|
| **1789** | Charter della North Carolina General Assembly |
| **1795** | Primo studente ammesso |
| **1945** | Creazione Morehead Scholarship (prima borsa di merito USA) |
| **1958** | Fondazione Triangle Institute for Security Studies (TISS) |
| **1982** | Dennis Gillings fonda Quintiles (CRO) da ricerca UNC |
| **2000** | Robertson Scholars Program (Julian Robertson, $24M) - programma congiunto UNC-Duke |
| **2007** | Gillings dona $50M, la School of Public Health prende il suo nome |
| **2014** | TISS designato Intelligence Community Center for Academic Excellence (DoD) |
| **2022** | Nancy Messonnier (ex CDC/Skoll) diventa Dean della Gillings School |
| **2024** | Lee H. Roberts nominato 13o Chancellor |

## Centri di Potere

### Gillings School of Global Public Health

Ranked #1 public school of public health (US News 2025-2026).

| Elemento | Dettaglio |
|----------|-----------|
| **Naming donor** | Dennis Gillings ($50M, 2007) - fondatore Quintiles/IQVIA |
| **Dean** | Nancy Messonnier (2022-) - ex CDC NCIRD Director, ex Skoll Foundation |
| **NIH Antiviral Center** | $65M grant per sviluppo antivirali orali pandemici |
| **Global reach** | Institute for Global Health and Infectious Diseases, attivo in 50+ paesi |

**Pipeline pandemica**: Gillings (Quintiles/IQVIA pharma) -> Messonnier (CDC/Skoll pandemic preparedness) -> NIH biodefense -> Ralph Baric (coronavirus research).
